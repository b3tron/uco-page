For the best-looking result:
• Dimensions: 1920×1080 or larger, landscape (16:9). Smaller images will look soft when stretched.
• Format: JPG, PNG, or WebP. Keep it under about 3 MB so the page stays fast.
• Style: subtle, low-contrast, or lightly textured images read best. A translucent paper overlay is applied automatically so text stays readable — use the slider below to tune it.

Enter the courses you're considering, pulled from GOLD. Each course can have several possible Lectures, and each lecture can have several Sections (discussion/lab). The optimizer will only build schedules from combinations that don't overlap in time.

Stands in for the rating when a lecture has none entered. 0 would penalize unrated professors as if confirmed bad; too high risks rewarding a real 1-star average. 3.0 (the default) treats "no data" as neutral — adjust to match how you'd want to gamble on an unknown.

Building Coordinates

Walking times between back-to-back classes are computed from real building coordinates. Buildings pulled from your courses appear below. A few are pre-filled; for the rest, click "Find on map", right-click the building's pin in Google Maps to copy its coordinates, and paste them here — every course in that building will use them automatically.

Background & Appearance

Drop in your own background image (a campus photo, a subtle texture, anything you like). A readable overlay is kept on top automatically, and the image never shows up on printed plans.

Data Integrity & Self-Tests

Runs built-in checks against the validator, the conflict detector, the walking-distance estimator, and the GOLD/description parsers using known-answer test cases. Also scans your current courses for anything that looks malformed (bad times, out-of-range ratings, seats open > seats total, etc).

Course Selection

Uncheck "In plans" to park a course without deleting it. Check "Required" to force a course into every plan — useful together with a course limit below your total course count, so the optimizer fills the remaining slots from your other included courses.
